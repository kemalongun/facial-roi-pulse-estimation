# References

The following papers were reviewed during the development of this project. They cover remote photoplethysmography (rPPG), imaging photoplethysmography (iPPG), chrominance-based pulse extraction, motion robustness, ROI-based facial video analysis, and video-based physiological monitoring.

1. Wang, W., den Brinker, A. C., Stuijk, S., & de Haan, G.  
   **Algorithmic Principles of Remote PPG.**  
   IEEE Transactions on Biomedical Engineering, 2017.

2. de Haan, G., & Jeanne, V.  
   **Robust Pulse Rate From Chrominance-Based rPPG.**  
   IEEE Transactions on Biomedical Engineering, 2013.

3. Song, R., Zhang, S., Cheng, J., Li, C., & Chen, X.  
   **New Insights on Super-High Resolution for Video-Based Heart Rate Estimation with a Semi-Blind Source Separation Method.**  
   Computers in Biology and Medicine, 2020.

4. Xing, W., Shi, Y., Wu, C., Wang, Y., & Wang, X.  
   **Predicting Blood Pressure from Face Videos Using Face Diagnosis Theory and Deep Neural Networks Technique.**  
   Computers in Biology and Medicine, 2023.

5. Song, R., Chen, H., Cheng, J., Li, C., Liu, Y., & Chen, X.  
   **PulseGAN: Learning to Generate Realistic Pulse Waveforms in Remote Photoplethysmography.**  
   IEEE Journal of Biomedical and Health Informatics, 2021.

6. Zhang, Q., Lin, X., Zhang, Y., Liu, Q., & Cai, F.  
   **Non-Contact High Precision Pulse-Rate Monitoring System for Moving Subjects in Different Motion States.**  
   Medical & Biological Engineering & Computing, 2023.

7. Wei, B., He, X., Zhang, C., & Wu, X.  
   **Non-Contact, Synchronous Dynamic Measurement of Respiratory Rate and Heart Rate Based on Dual Sensitive Regions.**  
   BioMedical Engineering OnLine, 2017.

8. Hamoud, B., Kashevnik, A., Othman, W., & Shilov, N.  
   **Neural Network Model Combination for Video-Based Blood Pressure Estimation: New Approach and Evaluation.**  
   Sensors, 2023.

9. Cheng, H., Xiong, J., Chen, Z., & Chen, J.  
   **Deep Learning-Based Non-Contact IPPG Signal Blood Pressure Measurement Research.**  
   Sensors, 2023.

10. Zhang, C., Wu, X., Zhang, L., He, X., & Lv, Z.  
    **Simultaneous Detection of Blink and Heart Rate Using Multi-Channel ICA from Smartphone Videos.**  
    Biomedical Signal Processing and Control, 2017.

11. Zhou, Y., Ni, H., Zhang, Q., & Wu, Q.  
    **The Noninvasive Blood Pressure Measurement Based on Facial Images Processing.**  
    IEEE Sensors Journal, 2019.

12. Ouzar, Y., Djeldjli, D., Bousefsaf, F., & Maaoui, C.  
    **X-iPPGNet: A Novel One-Stage Deep Learning Architecture Based on Depthwise Separable Convolutions for Video-Based Pulse Rate Estimation.**  
    Computers in Biology and Medicine, 2023.

13. Wu, B.-F., Wu, B.-J., Tsai, B.-R., & Hsu, C.-P.  
    **A Facial-Image-Based Blood Pressure Measurement System Without Calibration.**  
    IEEE Transactions on Instrumentation and Measurement, 2022.

14. Luo, H., Yang, D., Barszczyk, A., Vempala, N., Wei, J., Wu, S. J., Zheng, P. P., Fu, G., Lee, K., & Feng, Z.-P.  
    **Smartphone-Based Blood Pressure Measurement Using Transdermal Optical Imaging Technology.**  
    Circulation: Cardiovascular Imaging, 2019.

15. Wu, Y.-C., Chiu, L.-W., Wu, B.-F., Lin, L.-C., Ho, T.-H., Chung, M.-L., & Wu, S.-F.  
    **Motion Robust Remote Photoplethysmography Measurement During Exercise for Contactless Physical Activity Intensity Detection.**  
    IEEE Transactions on Instrumentation and Measurement, 2023.
